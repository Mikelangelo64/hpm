HIGH PRECISION MEASUREMENTS

HPM represents a team of specialists with 20+ years of experience in leading engineering positions in key core enterprises. We are dedicated to satisfying the needs of every customer, regardless of order complexity or volume. Our focus is on both mass production and sales of standard specifications strain gauges, as well as the manufacture of custom-made equipment. It is our goal to supply high quality equipment within a reasonable time and with loyal price policy. Cooperation with HPM is an obvious advantage for every customer.
